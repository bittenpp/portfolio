VISIT KOREA

UI & UX Design
Web · Mobile App · Design System
2022.04 - 2022.05 (3weeks)

Visit Korea is a website where travelers will be able to get
information for their travel needs. Travelers can get all possible
stories about travel sites and events in a way to experience Korea
more friendly and more deeply inside.

Concept

"Tour Guide for Tourists"
Visit Korea helps tourists get information about famous tour site, special events, local festival and even transportation.

User Research

I conducted a quick survey for 5 people
who are interested in travel, especially
planning to visit Korea.

Question

What’s the most important factor you take into
consideration while planning to visit other countries
for traveling?

Answer

Transportation · Travel Site · Weather Information ·
Travel Theme · Culture · Accommodations · Others

User Persona

To start the project, I created persona to explore the target group
and design my web and app with more closely to users.

Calvin Klein

Age : 35
Status : Single
Occupation : Data Scientist
Location : London, UK
Calvin is a data scientist. Who loves to travel around the world, experience various cultures. He usually finds local sites for his trip and enjoys the mood. He wants to get as much as information for tourists and to find attractive tour sites.

Goal

· Explore new sites and culture
· To receive information about
- transportation
· To get trip details and events plan
· Planning comfort travel

Influences

· Online & Social Media
· Friends & Colleagues

Frustration

· Difficult to find sources
· Hard to find genuine reviews
· Gets unclassified information
· Hard to get up-to-date events

Personality

· Friendly
· Adventurous
· Outgoing
